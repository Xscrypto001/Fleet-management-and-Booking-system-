Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our custom email domain service.

How does custom email domain work?

A custom email domain allows you to use your business name in your email address (you@yourbusiness.com) instead of using a generic provider domain (you@gmail.com). Our service handles all the technical details to connect your domain to our email servers or your preferred email provider (like Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, or Apple iCloud). This gives your business a more professional appearance and builds trust with your customers.

Do I need technical knowledge to set this up?

Not at all! Our guided setup wizard walks you through the entire process with simple, step-by-step instructions. Most users complete the setup in under 5 minutes. If you encounter any issues, our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you. We've designed the process to be accessible for everyone, regardless of technical expertise.

Can I use my existing email provider?

Yes! We support seamless integration with all major email providers including Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Apple iCloud, and many others. You can continue using your favorite email apps and services while enjoying the benefits of a custom domain. Alternatively, you can use our premium email hosting service which includes advanced features like enhanced security, spam protection, and large storage capacity.

How many email addresses can I create?

Our Starter plan includes up to 5 email addresses, which is perfect for small businesses and solopreneurs. Our Business plan supports up to 25 email addresses, while our Enterprise plan offers unlimited email addresses for your entire organization. You can easily upgrade your plan as your business grows, without any disruption to your existing email accounts.

What if I don't have a domain yet?

No problem! We offer domain registration services directly through our platform. You can search for available domains, purchase them, and immediately set up your custom email addresses all in one place. We also provide domain management tools to help you maintain your online presence effectively. Our premium plans even include a free domain for the first year.
